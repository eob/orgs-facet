Who Occupies?

About
This is a faceted browser for the results of the Occupy Research General Demographics and Participation Survey done at the Cambridge location of the Occupy Data 2 Hackathon. Thanks to Charlie DeTar for cleaning up the dataset. It uses the Exhibit framework as a facet engine, and you can find the code on GitHub.

How to use
Expand the survey questions you are interested in and click the checkboxes to filter the data. For example, to see the reasons for participation for females, expand the Gender and Why do you participate? boxes, and then click the checkbox for Female. Then observe the updated counts in the Why do you participate? box.

Note 1: The dataset is rather large (2MB) so allow for a while to load and responsd to facet changes

Note 2: Several fields from the survey (political party, participation, voting habits) aren't displayed here. Help us develop a matrix-style filter widget to show them!
